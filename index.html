Curiosity is a rover designed to explore Gale Crater on Mars as part of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory mission.

Mission Objectives

Determine if Mars can, or has ever supported life.

Study the climate and geology of Mars.

Curiosity launched November 26, 2011 and landed August 6, 2012.

Since landing, the rover has navigated 11.19 miles.

Over 450,000 photos have been taken among its 17 different cameras.
